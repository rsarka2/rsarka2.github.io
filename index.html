About Me

I'm an undergraduate at Boston University studying Biomedical Engineering with a minor in Chemistry and a concentration in Nanotechnology, enrolled in the Kilachand Honors College.

My research focuses on nanotechnology — specifically analyzing antibody attachment orientation on metal-organic framework nanoparticles to understand how orientation impacts cellular uptake. I am passionate about leveraging nanoscale engineering to advance drug delivery and other biomedical applications.

Alongside the lab, I'm an Engineering Intern at CareAI in Sydney, developing AI-powered document classification systems for clinical data. I'm eager to bring my interdisciplinary skill set to innovative projects at the intersection of biomedical engineering and technology.
